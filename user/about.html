About Us

Welcome to Vizhinjam International Seaport

The Vizhinjam International Seaport, located near Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, is India’s first deep-water multipurpose seaport with a natural depth of over 20 meters. Strategically positioned just 10 nautical miles from international shipping routes, it is designed to be a world-class transshipment hub.

Our E-SEA PORTAL digital platform enables users, companies, and contractors to seamlessly access port services online — including registrations, tender participation, cargo tracking, and complaint management.
